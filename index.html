IIPL is a Rajkot (India) based consortium of web and software development. Since 2004, IIPL is helping businesses do better with digital solutions. In the last 9 years, IIPL has built a strong clientele base across the countries based on our commitment to deliver solutions that work in unique ways. This confidence has come about with sincere perseverance, constant upgradation of applied technology, and incisive experience & project management capabilities built over the years.
It is oft repeated but irresistible to say that the world is fast defined as digitally enabled and digitally developing. In the present world scenario, much of the development is coming from the economic growth of Asian countries—China with its manufacturing might and expertise; India with the soft skills. IIPL is at the forefront of exploiting the key growth drivers in the Indian development story—Internet Technology. Yet it needs more pointed explanation of what IIPL’s core strength is.
We believe that ideas and businesses deserve customized solutions and personal attention to grow. Our motto 'For your ideas, we have infinite solutions' reflects our core understanding that no single solution fits all. First and foremost is to understand your business’s action flow and its complete circle of source and supply. This understanding helps in creating correct options and presenting them as the first step. Once we have your feedback, we consolidate from the foundation points and develop a complete solution. Of course, regular updates and interactions with the clients ensure that the solutions are focused and each detail has its appropriate place and effectiveness
